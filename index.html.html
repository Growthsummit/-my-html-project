About the Event

You Matter.
Let's Talk About It.

Mental Health Awareness Day is a dedicated space for open, honest conversations about mental well-being. Hosted by Unfiltered Minds, this event brings together community members, wellness advocates, and those who simply want to feel less alone.

Whether you're on a healing journey, supporting someone you love, or just curious — you belong here. Together we can create a healthier tomorrow, one conversation at a time.

No judgment. No pressure. Just real talk, real support, and real change.
